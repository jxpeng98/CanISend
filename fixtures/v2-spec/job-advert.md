# Lecturer in Economics

Northbridge University is seeking a Lecturer in Economics. The successful applicant will contribute to undergraduate
and postgraduate teaching, maintain an active research programme, and support the academic community.

## Essential criteria

1. A completed PhD in Economics or a closely related discipline.
2. Evidence of effective university-level teaching.
3. A developing record of peer-reviewed research in applied economics.
4. Ability to communicate research clearly to specialist and non-specialist audiences.

## Desirable criteria

1. Experience teaching econometrics.
2. Evidence of collaborative research.

## Application materials

Applicants must provide a CV, a cover letter addressing the criteria, and a two-page research statement. Shortlisted
candidates will be invited to give a research seminar and teaching presentation.

The closing date is 31 August 2026. This synthetic advert contains no real vacancy or personal data.
